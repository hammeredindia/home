About Hammered

Hammered is an adult party game that ironically stands by but also violates all the 'Live and Let Live' conditions.
The card game makes for a wild party, perfect for Bar Games, couple games, bachelorette party Games, Bachelor Parties, New Year Parties, House Parties, etc.
We are here to give your traditional drinking games a new twist.
There are two formats of cards in the game:
a) Regular Cards: You Pull out the card follow the instructions written on the card or drink.
b) Truth Cards: These cards demand a story from you, failing to narrate the story will lead you to a severe penalty.
An interesting element in the game is the DRUNKEN GOD, make sure to stay by the promise you made him else face his wrath
Hammered will end the search for indoor games and card games for your party is over The Game includes a deck of 110 Fun Cards, a promise to get HAMMERED and have a legendary time with your friends.

How to Play

RULES

Take turns to draw cards from the deck moving in a clockwise direction read the card out loud and follow the instructions on each card to determine who has to drink.


TRUTH CARDS


The Person Drawing the truth card has to answer the question/situation truthfully.The person denying to answer the question has to down their drink. The card holder also gets to select one person who will answer the same question.
